# Case Study 1: Daily Food Budget

Scenario: student caps food spending at HK$50/day. Data contains 7 days with some days exceeding.

Expected behavior:

- Alerts about Food daily amounts >50.
- Summary shows days and totals correctly.

Files:

- `case1_transactions.csv`
- `case1_rules.json`
- `case1_recurring_rules.json`

How to run:

- Copy the corresponding files into `data/transactions.csv`, `data/recurring_rules.json` and `data/budget_rules.json` and restart the app.
